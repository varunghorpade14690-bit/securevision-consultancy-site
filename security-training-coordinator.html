Security Training Coordinator

Part-time | Hybrid | Location: Ireland

Apply Now

Role Overview

Deliver practical security awareness and preparedness programs for client teams. You will translate security procedures into clear training modules, drills, and role-based guidance that improves on-ground response quality.

Responsibilities

  • Design and run security awareness sessions for staff and supervisors.
  • Create drill plans for evacuation, intrusion, and emergency escalation scenarios.
  • Track training completion and readiness KPIs by site and team.
  • Maintain role-based playbooks and procedural reference materials.
  • Support post-incident learning sessions and improvement recommendations.

Required Experience

  • Experience in training delivery, security operations, or facilities support roles.
  • Strong communication, facilitation, and documentation skills.
  • Ability to engage mixed audiences from frontline teams to management.
