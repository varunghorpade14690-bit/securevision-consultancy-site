Security Operations Consultant

Full-time | Hybrid | Location: Ireland

Apply Now

Role Overview

Lead operational security consulting assignments from assessment to execution planning. You will work directly with facility managers, operations leaders, and field teams to build practical controls that reduce security risk without disrupting daily business operations.

Responsibilities

  • Perform site security assessments and risk prioritization.
  • Design security operating procedures and escalation workflows.
  • Define guard deployment models and response readiness plans.
  • Coordinate with surveillance and access control teams on implementation.
  • Present findings and action plans to client stakeholders.

Required Experience

  • Experience in physical security operations or consulting delivery.
  • Strong understanding of site controls, patrol processes, and incident handling.
  • Clear communication and client-facing presentation skills.
